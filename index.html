How to Use:

  1. Create a bot account on Kick.com
  2. Enter your bot credentials above
  3. Select your target language (messages in this language won't be translated)
  4. Enter the Kick channel URL you want to monitor
  5. Click "Start Translation Bot"
  6. The bot will automatically translate non-English messages and reply in chat
Note: This bot runs entirely in your browser and is completely free. Make sure to keep this page open while the bot is running.
